Anthropic investigating claim of unauthorised access to Mythos AI tool

Anthropic says it is investigating claims of unauthorised access to Mythos, its AI system that it has described as too dangerous to release publicly because of its hacking capabilities.

Anthropic says it is investigating claims of unauthorised access to Mythos, its AI system that it has described as too dangerous to release publicly because of its hacking capabilities.

The allegation matters because it involves a tool that the company itself has said should not be widely deployed, making security around the model especially sensitive.

If the claim is confirmed, it could intensify concerns about how advanced AI systems are controlled, who can reach them and what risks emerge if they are misused.

The episode also adds to a wider debate in the AI industry over whether some capabilities are becoming too powerful to manage safely with current safeguards.

Anthropic has not said how serious the alleged access was, and that uncertainty will shape attention in the days ahead.
