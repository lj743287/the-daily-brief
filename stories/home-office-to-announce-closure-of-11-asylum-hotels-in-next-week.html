Home Office to announce closure of 11 asylum hotels in next week

The Home Office is expected to close 11 asylum hotels as part of Labour’s pledge to end routine use of hotels for asylum accommodation. The announcement will be closely watched by councils, local residents and migration campaigners.

Ministers are moving to reduce asylum hotel use, a politically sensitive issue that has become a flashpoint for both local pressure and national migration politics. The key question is whether closures can be sustained without simply shifting the problem elsewhere.
