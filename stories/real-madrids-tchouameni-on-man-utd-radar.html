Real Madrid's Tchouameni on Man Utd radar

Real Madrid midfielder Aurélien Tchouaméni is among the players Manchester United are looking at as they target a central midfield signing this summer.

Manchester United are looking at Real Madrid midfielder Aurélien Tchouaméni as they plan for a central midfield signing this summer.

The interest matters because United’s midfield has been a recurring area of concern, and a player of Tchouaméni’s profile would signal a serious attempt to add quality and control.

Real Madrid are unlikely to make any deal straightforward, so this is very much one name on a list rather than a completed pursuit.

Even so, the link offers an early indication of the kind of player United may target: proven at the top level, physically strong and capable of strengthening the spine of the side.

As ever with summer transfer talk, the main uncertainty is whether interest becomes a genuine bid.
