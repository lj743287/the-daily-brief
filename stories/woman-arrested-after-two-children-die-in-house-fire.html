Woman arrested after two children die in house fire

Police in Wolverhampton have arrested a woman after two boys, aged one and three, died in a house fire. West Midlands Police are continuing their inquiries into the cause of the blaze.

Police in Wolverhampton have arrested a woman after two boys, aged one and three, died in a house fire.

West Midlands Police said the children died at the scene and that a woman is in custody as inquiries continue into the cause of the blaze.

The case is being treated with extreme seriousness because fires involving very young children often prompt urgent questions about how the incident started and whether it could have been prevented.

At this stage, police have not said what caused the fire or given further details about the woman who was arrested, so the investigation remains at an early stage.

For the local area, the immediate priority will be supporting those affected and establishing the facts as quickly as possible.

Any criminal proceedings would depend on what detectives uncover, but for now the case remains a tragic and unresolved fire investigation.
