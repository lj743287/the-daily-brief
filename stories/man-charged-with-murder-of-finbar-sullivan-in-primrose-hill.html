Man charged with murder of Finbar Sullivan in Primrose Hill

Police charged a 27‑year‑old man following the fatal stabbing of the 21‑year‑old in north London. The suspect is due in court as detectives appeal for further information.

A 27‑year‑old man has been charged with the murder of 21‑year‑old Finbar Sullivan, who died after a stabbing in Primrose Hill, north London, according to the BBC and the Guardian.

The Metropolitan police named the suspect as Oliuwadamilola Ogunyankinnu, of Enfield, who is due to appear at Stratford magistrates’ court on Monday, the Guardian reported.

Emergency services were called to the scene following reports of a stabbing, but Mr Sullivan could not be saved despite treatment.

Detectives have appealed for witnesses and information to assist their inquiries as they piece together the events leading up to the attack.

Officers have increased patrols in the area to reassure residents and gather further intelligence while specialist teams examine potential forensic leads.

Police have urged anyone with dashcam or mobile phone footage from the vicinity to contact the force or provide information anonymously via Crimestoppers.
