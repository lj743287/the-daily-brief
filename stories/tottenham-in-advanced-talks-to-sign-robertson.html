Tottenham in advanced talks to sign Robertson

Tottenham are in advanced talks over a free transfer for Andy Robertson, a notable Premier League move with Liverpool’s defender potentially available this summer.

Tottenham are in advanced talks to sign Andy Robertson on a free transfer, a move that would be one of the more notable Premier League deals of the summer.

Robertson’s possible availability has given Spurs a chance to strengthen a key area without paying a fee, but the transfer would still be a significant statement about their ambitions and Liverpool’s squad transition.

For a player of Robertson’s experience, any move would carry immediate sporting weight as well as symbolic value. He has been a central figure in Liverpool’s recent success, so his departure would mark a shift in the balance of the side.

The main uncertainty is whether talks progress to an agreement and how much competition Tottenham face if the deal opens up.

If it happens, it would be one of the headline defensive moves of the window.
