Jannik Sinner defeats Carlos Alcaraz to reclaim world number one

Italy’s Jannik Sinner beat Spain’s Carlos Alcaraz in the Monte Carlo final to return to the top of the men’s rankings. The result cements Sinner’s rise and underscores a shifting balance at the summit of men’s tennis. With the clay season under way, the ranking battle will frame the run-up to Roland Garros. The rivalry is fast becoming the sport’s defining contest.
