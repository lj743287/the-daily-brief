Man City smell blood - why Arsenal should fear title rivals

Manchester City’s latest win has tightened the Premier League title race and put Arsenal under renewed pressure in the run-in.

Manchester City’s latest win has tightened the Premier League title race and put Arsenal under renewed pressure in the run-in.

The broader point is not just that City are winning, but that they are doing so with the familiar relentlessness that has defined their most successful seasons. Once they sense momentum, they rarely give rivals much room to recover.

Arsenal now have to absorb the psychological effect of being chased by a side that has made late-season pressure its speciality. With each dropped point becoming more costly, the title race is moving into the kind of stretch where City usually make the difference.
