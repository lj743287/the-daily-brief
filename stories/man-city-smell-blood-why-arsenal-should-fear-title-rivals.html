Man City smell blood - why Arsenal should fear title rivals

Manchester City’s 3-0 win over Chelsea has tightened the Premier League title race and strengthened the view that Pep Guardiola’s side are Arsenal’s most dangerous pursuers.

Manchester City's 3-0 win over Chelsea has tightened the Premier League title race and made Pep Guardiola's side Arsenal's most dangerous pursuers. The result was as much a statement of intent as a three-point swing.

City have now capitalised on Arsenal's latest slip-up, and the performance suggested they are hitting form at the right moment. In a title race where momentum matters, that can be decisive.

For Arsenal, the warning is obvious: even small errors now have the potential to become costly. With the pressure rising and the margin narrowing, City's experience in closing out seasons makes them formidable opponents.
