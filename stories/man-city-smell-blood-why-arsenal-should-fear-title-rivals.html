Man City smell blood - why Arsenal should fear title rivals

Manchester City have capitalised on Arsenal’s slip and are closing in on the Premier League title race. The focus now is less on one weekend’s result than on whether City can sustain the pressure to the finish.

Manchester City’s latest win has tightened the Premier League title race and shifted the mood around the run-in.

Arsenal’s wobble has made the lead look less secure, while City have found form at the right time and appear to be building momentum again. In a title race this close, that combination can matter as much as the points themselves.

The key period now is the direct stretch between the front-runners and their closest challengers. If Arsenal drop more points, City have the experience and squad depth to make the pressure tell quickly.

For Arsenal, the concern is not just City’s quality but the psychology of a chase. They have to hold their nerve while rivals sense vulnerability, and that can become decisive long before the final whistle of the season.
