Man City smell blood - why Arsenal should fear title rivals

Manchester City’s win over Chelsea has turned up the pressure on Arsenal, with the title race now feeling far less secure for the leaders.

Manchester City’s latest win has tightened the Premier League title race and put fresh pressure on Arsenal. After a strong run from the leaders, Pep Guardiola’s side have now made it clear they are closing in.

The significance is not just the three points but the timing. When City start to build momentum, rivals know how quickly a lead can disappear, especially with a game in hand and a squad used to handling late-season pressure.

For Arsenal, the warning is obvious: any slip now invites scrutiny and doubt. In a title race, that can matter as much as the numbers on the table.
