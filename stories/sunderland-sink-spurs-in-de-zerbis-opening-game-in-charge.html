Sunderland sink Spurs in De Zerbi's opening game in charge

Roberto De Zerbi’s first match as Tottenham manager ended in defeat at Sunderland, leaving Spurs in the relegation zone. The result deepens the club’s crisis and raises immediate questions about survival.

Roberto De Zerbi’s first match as Tottenham manager ended in a 1-0 defeat at Sunderland, leaving Spurs in the relegation zone. Nordi Mukiele scored the only goal in a result that will intensify pressure on a club already under severe strain.

The immediate significance is obvious: this was meant to be a fresh start, and instead it became another setback. De Zerbi will be judged not on long-term ideas but on whether he can quickly stabilise a side short on confidence and points, with every remaining fixture carrying relegation implications.

There is still time to recover, but little margin for error. Tottenham’s problems are no longer hypothetical; they are structural and immediate, with the club now forced to respond not just to a bad result, but to the prospect of a damaging slide that could define the season.
