At least 30 feared dead in crush at Haitian tourist site

At least 30 people are feared dead after a crush at a tourist event in Haiti, where the disaster struck an event attended by many young people.

At least 30 people are feared dead after a crush at a tourist event in Haiti, where officials say many of those caught up in the disaster were young people. The incident took place at a popular site that had drawn a large crowd.

The country's prime minister said the event was attended by many young people, underlining the scale of the tragedy and the vulnerability of large public gatherings in Haiti, where emergency services are often overstretched. The number of dead could rise as more information comes in from the scene.

The disaster adds to a long list of crises facing Haiti, including insecurity, weak public institutions and repeated strain on health and rescue services. Authorities will now be under pressure to explain how crowd control failed and whether more lives could have been saved.
