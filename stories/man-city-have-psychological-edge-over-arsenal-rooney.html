Man City have psychological edge over Arsenal - Rooney

Wayne Rooney says Manchester City now hold the psychological edge over Arsenal in the Premier League title race after their latest win over Chelsea.

Wayne Rooney believes Manchester City now hold the psychological edge over Arsenal in the Premier League title race. That view follows City’s latest win over Chelsea, which has increased the pressure on the leaders as the season enters its decisive stretch.

The idea of a psychological edge matters in a title race because points are only part of the story. When rivals keep winning and the frontrunner starts to slip, confidence can shift quickly, and the burden of expectation can become as important as form.

City’s recent surge has made Arsenal’s position more precarious. With every good result, Pep Guardiola’s side can present themselves as the team with momentum, experience and the habit of handling pressure at the right time.

Rooney’s point is not that the title is decided, but that the balance of belief may already be changing. In a close contest, that can be decisive long before the maths is settled.
