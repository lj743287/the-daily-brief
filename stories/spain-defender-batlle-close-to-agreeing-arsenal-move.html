Spain defender Batlle close to agreeing Arsenal move

Spain defender Ona Batlle is close to agreeing a summer move to Arsenal as a free agent, a potentially important addition for the women’s side.

Spain defender Ona Batlle is close to agreeing a move to Arsenal as a free agent in the summer.

The possible signing would be an important addition for Arsenal’s women’s side, giving them experience and quality in a position where elite depth matters over a long season.

Because Batlle would arrive on a free transfer, the move could also represent strong value if the deal is completed.

The broader significance is that top women’s clubs are now competing aggressively for proven international players, and Arsenal remain intent on staying among Europe’s leading sides.

The deal is not yet final, but it appears to be moving in the right direction.
