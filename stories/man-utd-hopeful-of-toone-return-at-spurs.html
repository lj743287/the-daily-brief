Man Utd hopeful of Toone return at Spurs

Manchester United midfielder Ella Toone could return after four months out when United travel to Tottenham on Sunday.

Manchester United midfielder Ella Toone could return from injury when United visit Tottenham on Sunday, with the club hopeful she will train on Friday.

Toone has been out for four months, so even being close to selection is significant for United as they approach a key fixture. Her return would give the side more control and creativity in midfield.

The news matters because injuries can shape the closing stages of a season as much as tactics do. For United, getting an experienced player back could improve their chances in a game with real implications for their league position.

The final decision will depend on how Toone comes through training and how cautious the club wants to be. But the signs are encouraging.
