Where does McIlroy rank among greats after Masters defence?

After winning the Masters again, Rory McIlroy is now being compared with the game’s all-time greats as he closes in on the major-victory records held by golf’s elite.

Rory McIlroy's Masters defence has renewed the debate over where he sits among golf's greats. With a rare repeat win at Augusta and a career Grand Slam already complete, he is moving closer to the game's record-holders.

The comparison now stretches beyond Europe. McIlroy has joined a very short list of players who have managed to retain the Masters, and each major he adds strengthens the case that he belongs among the sport's defining figures.

What remains open is how far he can go from here. The all-time major record is still some way off, but with form, experience and momentum on his side, the discussion has clearly shifted from whether he is great to how great he may yet become.
