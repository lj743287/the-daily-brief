McIlroy proves he really is free with back-to-back Masters win

Rory McIlroy’s repeat Masters victory gives golf its most significant weekend result and cements one of the sport’s defining recent achievements.

Rory McIlroy’s second straight Masters victory is one of the defining results of modern golf and a rare repeat triumph at Augusta National.

The significance goes beyond another major title. After years in which McIlroy’s Augusta narrative was shaped by disappointment and near-misses, retaining the green jacket confirms that his first win was not a one-off but the start of a new phase in his career.

It also strengthens his place among the game’s leading figures of the era. Back-to-back victories at the Masters are exceptionally hard to achieve, and doing so under pressure will only deepen the sense that McIlroy has finally settled into the role that eluded him for so long.
