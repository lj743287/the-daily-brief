Will Romero's tears become the enduring image of Tottenham's season?

Cristian Romero’s reaction after Tottenham’s defeat at Sunderland has become a symbol of a difficult season that now leaves Spurs in the relegation zone.

Cristian Romero's tears after Tottenham's defeat at Sunderland have come to symbolise a season that has unravelled badly. The loss left Spurs in the relegation zone and deepened the sense of crisis around the club.

The image is powerful because it captures more than one bad result: it reflects the weight of expectation, frustration and uncertainty now hanging over Tottenham. When a captain or senior defender reacts that openly, it says something about the scale of the pressure inside the dressing room.

The new manager faces an immediate fight to steady the club, but the wider problem is structural. Unless results improve quickly, Romero's tears may indeed become the defining picture of a season Spurs will want to forget.
