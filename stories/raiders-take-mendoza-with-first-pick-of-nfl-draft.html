Raiders take Mendoza with first pick of NFL Draft

The Las Vegas Raiders selected Fernando Mendoza first overall in the NFL Draft, making him only the third player to be a Heisman winner, a national champion and No 1 pick in the same year.

The Las Vegas Raiders have selected Fernando Mendoza with the first pick in the NFL Draft, making him the latest quarterback to arrive with huge expectations.

Mendoza becomes only the third player in history to be a Heisman Trophy winner, a national champion and the No 1 overall pick in the same year. That combination alone underlines how highly the Raiders rate him.

The selection matters because quarterback picks at the top of the draft often define a franchise for years, for better or worse. For the Raiders, it is a clear bet that Mendoza can become the centrepiece of their long-term rebuild.

The pressure will now shift immediately to performance and development. Being drafted first is one thing; becoming the player the team expects is another.
