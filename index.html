Hormuz at the brink: Trump’s blockade threat collides with failed US–Iran talks and a tightening energy squeeze

Donald Trump’s threat to blockade the Strait of Hormuz after US–Iran talks collapsed raises the risk of confrontation without resolving the underlying stalemate.
